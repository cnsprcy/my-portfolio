My Wedding Invitations

Invitations

I designed our own wedding and bridal shower invitations.

● Parts handdrawn in Procreate and designed and finalized with Adobe Photoshop



Overview


Our wedding was a earthy, bohemean, alice in wonderland feel. My wife loves all things flowers, plants, vintage, and colorful. So she had a few ideas on what she wanted for our invites and let me choose how to incorperate it all together!

● For our bridal shower she asked for the tea party scene and watercolor. I added some lace to the top to add a vintage flair. Then incorperated watercolored plants and drops throughout the tea party scene to give a fun pop of color.

● For the wedding invitations we had a thought of doing a mirror with a floral border, similar to the drawing style of old Alice in Wonderland, along with more watercolor to tie everything together!
