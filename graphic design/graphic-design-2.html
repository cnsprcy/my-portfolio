Silverleaf Stables

Logo Design

Logo design for a family owned horse stable business located in central Florida.

● Handdrawn in Procreate, modified with Adobe Illustrator.



Overview


The client originally asked for a logo that included a silverleaf tree, a horse grazing on grass, cursive lettering, along with their established date. They were also open to my interpretation of what they wanted. The overall feeling that they were going for was elegance.

● I chose to give them a few options to see if they were open to a typography forward approach over an image based logo.

● Instead of doing a tree and a horse, I decided to add flowing silverleaf leaves and a horse shoe to keep things light and airy.
