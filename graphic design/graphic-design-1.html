The Intuitive Rock

Logo and Web Design

Logo and web design for a family owned metaphysical business.

● Handdrawn in Procreate.

● Wireframe designed with Figma.



Overview


The client wanted a logo that spoke to her intuitive side. One that was full of cool, calming colors like an aurora; yet still super inviting and full of curiosity that would drive people in. We narrowed down the must haves for her logo which were crystals, plant life, intuition (of course), and something that would look awesome on a shirt or sticker!

● She loved the idea of one opalized and one super colorful crystal. This is where I used the aurora borealis as inspiration in the colors that I chose.

● One of her all time favorite plants is the beautiful prickly pear cactus, it also goes great with the cool tones. Plus, those mushrooms just add a great touch and help break things up!

● I chose to use a lightbulb and a third eye to signify intuition. The lightbulb for enlightenment and the third eye for clairvoyance.
