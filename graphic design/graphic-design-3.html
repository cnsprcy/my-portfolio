Logo Design

Logo design for a scaleless cornsnake breeder and seller.

● Handdrawn in Procreate.



Overview


The client was open to anything as long as it sold who their company was. I asked for pictures of their favorite snakes and started doing the thing!

● There was one snake they picked out in particular so I decided to showcase that one.

● I wanted to keep it alive and flow well together so I chose to do a banner with their company name flowing through the snake.
