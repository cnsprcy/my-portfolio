Riverstone Reserve

Logo Design

Logo design for a personal wine cellar.

● Designed with Adobe Illustrator.



Overview


The client wanted a logo for his wine cellar. He was extremely open to me doing any style so I chose to do a typeography forward design.

● He loved the idea of using cursive so we got to choose one of my favorite cursive fonts!

● The simplistic approach to the logo ended up the way to go so I kept it as minimal as possible without sacraficing the fun style.
